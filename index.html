Nunca foi tão urgente preservar a Natureza e nunca foi tão importante celebrar quem o faz.

incentivando as boas práticas ambientais.

The Future of Nature is the Best Present.

It has never been more urgent to preserve Nature, and it has never been more

important to celebrate those who do.

Through renewable energy, GENERG/NOVENERGIA values natural resources,

encouraging good environmental practices.

Thanks to enormous courage and dedication, the Volunteer Firefighters of Manteigas

help to preserve the natural heritage of Serra da Estrela.

This Christmas we want to express our gratitude and admiration for the mission

of the Soldiers of Peace, in the form of a donation.

On behalf of GENERG/NOVENERGIA and all those who love Serra da Estrela,

THANK YOU VERY MUCH.
